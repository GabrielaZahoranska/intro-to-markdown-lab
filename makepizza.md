# How to Make a Pizza

![Homemade Pizza](https://plus.unsplash.com/premium_photo-1673439304183-8840bd0dc1bf?ixlib=rb-4.1.0&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D&auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=687)

This guide walks through every step, from dough to slice.

## 1. **Gather Your Ingredients**

| Ingredient | Description | Example |
| ----------- | ------------ | -------- |
| Dough | Homemade or store-bought |
| Sauce | Tomato or pesto |
| Cheese | Mozzarella, cheddar |
| Toppings | Veggies, meat, herbs |

**Shopping List:**

- [x] Dough  
- [x] Sauce  
- [x] Cheese  
- [ ] Toppings of your choice  

> *Tip:* Fresh ingredients create the best texture and flavor.


## 2. **Prepare the Dough**

Stretch the dough into a circle around.

**Example:**
1. Dust surface with flour
2. Stretch dough by hand
3. Let rest 5 minutes if needed

## 3. Add the Sauce

Spread the sauce evenly across the dough.

**Steps**
text
1. Spoon sauce onto the center
2. Spread in circles toward the edges
3. Leave 1 inch of crust uncovered

> A thin layer of sauce prevents sogginess.

## 4. Add Cheese and Toppings

Layer cheese first, then add toppings evenly.

*Popular Cheeses:* **mozzarella**, *cheddar*,   
*Common Toppings:*
- Mushrooms  
- Peppers  
- Olives  
- Chicken  
- Basil  

**Steps**
text
1. Add cheese evenly
2. Place toppings across the pizza
3. Drizzle with olive oil

> Too many toppings make the crust undercooked.


## 5. Bake the Pizza

Preheat oven to `245°C`.  
Bake for **10–12 minutes** until the crust turns golden and the cheese bubbles.

**Steps**
text
1. Preheat oven
2. Place pizza on a hot baking sheet or stone
3. Bake for 10–12 minutes

> The ideal pizza has a crisp edge and soft center.


## 6. Slice and Serve

Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before cutting.  
Use a sharp knife or pizza wheel for clean slices.

**Optional toppings after baking**

- Salt and pepper  
- Chili flakes  
- Olive oil drizzle
